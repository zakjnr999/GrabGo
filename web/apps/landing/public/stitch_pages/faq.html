Refunds

Policy on canceled orders expand_more
Orders canceled before the vendor starts preparation are eligible for a full refund. If the vendor has already begun preparing your order, a partial refund or cancellation fee may apply depending on the specific store's policy. Refunds are typically issued back to the original payment method.
How long do refund processing times take? expand_more
Once approved, refunds usually take 3-5 business days to appear in your account, depending on your bank or credit card provider. Digital wallet refunds are often processed within 24 hours.

Late Deliveries

What if my order is late? expand_more
If your delivery is delayed past the estimated window, we recommend checking the live tracker in the app. If it's more than 20 minutes late, you may be eligible for a delivery fee credit or GrabGo points as an apology for the wait.
