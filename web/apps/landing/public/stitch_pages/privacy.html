1. Introduction

GrabGo ("we," "our," or "us") is dedicated to protecting your personal data. This Privacy Policy applies to our website, mobile applications, and all related services (collectively, the "Services") that connect customers, independent delivery partners ("Riders"), and merchant partners ("Vendors").

By using our Services, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy. We recommend reading this document in full to understand how we treat your information.

2. Data Collection

We collect several different types of information for various purposes to provide and improve our Services to you.

person Personal Information

  • Contact details (Name, email address, phone number).
  • Delivery addresses and saved locations.
  • Payment information (Processed securely via third-party providers).
  • Profile photos and account preferences.

location_on Location Data

Crucial for our delivery ecosystem:

  • Real-time GPS tracking for active deliveries (Riders and Customers).
  • Device-based location to suggest nearby Vendors.
  • Historical address data to improve logistics efficiency.

3. How We Use Information

GrabGo uses the collected data for various purposes, primarily focused on facilitating the delivery process:

  • Fulfillment: To process orders and ensure accurate delivery from Vendor to Customer.
  • Safety & Security: To verify identities of Riders and prevent fraudulent transactions.
  • Communication: To send order status updates and respond to customer support inquiries.
  • Product Improvement: To analyze usage patterns and optimize our delivery algorithms.
  • Marketing: With your consent, to provide news about promotions or new local Vendors.

4. Sharing Data

We share information within our ecosystem only when necessary to complete a transaction or provide a service:

With Vendors

We share your name and order details so merchants can prepare your items correctly.

With Riders

We share your address and phone number to facilitate safe and timely delivery.

5. User Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Access: The right to request copies of your personal data.
  • Correction: The right to request that we correct inaccurate information.
  • Deletion: The right to request that we erase your personal data under certain conditions.
  • Portability: The right to request that we transfer your data to another organization.

6. Cookie Policy

We use cookies and similar tracking technologies to track the activity on our Service and hold certain information. Cookies are files with a small amount of data which may include an anonymous unique identifier.
